## Buttons & tap targets per stage

- **Privacy**: 同意 (x=770, y=1715)
- **Login**: After filling credentials, tap `Login` at (234,909). (Note: the bottom‑zone `Login` at y=1993 is IME‑only, ignore.)
- **Server select**: Tap **any** server row (e.g. x=540, y=1493) → then `开始游戏` (central, tap at ~(539,2111) from OCR)
- **In‑game**: No further taps needed; check_in_game will confirm.

## Pitfalls & tips

1. **GameTurbo overlay** (`GT[HK]: Xms S:Y`) is always present; ignore it.
2. **Download progress** appears in the **keyboard/IME zone** (y≥1392). Do not tap that region. Wait until the progress text disappears entirely (not just reaches 100%).
3. **Login button** may not be cached automatically; call `get_ocr_summary` after password fill to confirm coordinates before `tap_coordinate`.
4. **Server select** is mandatory after login for this game – not covered in generic skill. Tap a server name (any), then `开始游戏`.
5. **Post‑login server maintenance** text (`server维护`) may appear briefly; it is **not** a blocking error – continue to wait for main scene.
6. **fill_credential_field** works – x,y from OCR of field labels. Both `username` and `password` fields were found and filled successfully (verified).
7. **No announcement modal** seen between privacy and download; if appearance differs, treat as unknown stage and `analyze_screen`.
8. **Check_in_game** was eventually positive – expected 2 consecutive confirms. Tutorial bubble (Katie) is part of in‑game main.
