## Typical pitfalls observed

1. **Login submit loop** – after password fill, the system tried ENTER and then dismissed keyboard; had to manually tap `Login` button to proceed.
2. **Download completion detection** – progress text can vanish while background network activity persists (upload/download KB/s still shown). Do not assume full game is ready; wait for an additional OCR that shows login screen.
3. **Field coordinates drift** – `Account` hint at (480,1128) and password placeholder at (452,1312) are reliable; use `tap_coordinate` before `fill_credential_field` to focus the correct element.
