## Stage order & key actions

1. **Splash** – no action; game process appears automatically.

2. **Privacy** – OCR keyword: `个人信息保护指引` — tap `同意` (coords 1671,968). No checkbox needed.

3. **Download (resource decompress)** – shows `正在解压资源（仅首次游戏需要，不消耗流量）(58/164)`. Wait for progress to change.

4. **Download (update file)** – OCR changes to `下载更新文件 1.63M/5.66M(x1.3.1.41749)`. Wait until this line disappears (~10 seconds).

5. **Login** – OCR: `Account, phone number, email`, `Login password`.  
   - Fill username field using hint coords (1681,295) via `fill_credential_field`.  
   - Fill password field using hint coords (1548,446) via `fill_credential_field`.  
   - System auto-submits (ENTER + dismiss + cache Login coords).

6. **Server sub-account selection** – After login, shows `Ariakagami39`, `Sub-account description`, `Sub-account1 (Last login)`. Tap `Sub-account1 (Last login)` at (1680,270).

7. **Announcement (x7game confirm)** – `Warm reminder` dialog: `The language pack will be loaded from x7game. Please agree to "Open x7game"`. Tap `Confirm` at (1465,681).

8. **Loading** – `Loading language pack from x7game..` appears; wait for completion. Then in-game HUD loads.

## Pitfalls

- Download progress text changes from decompress to update download; do not proceed until the download line disappears.  
- Password fill may mismatch field; system retries automatically.  
- The sub-account list may have multiple entries; always choose the one marked `(Last login)`.  
- After tapping Confirm on the warm reminder, a loading screen appears; wait for in-game HUD before calling `check_in_game`.  
- If a “Friendly Reminder” exit-confirmation dialog appears over in-game scene, the player is already in-game (success criterion met).
