## Buttons & tap targets per stage

- **Privacy**: `同意` at (770, 1715)
- **Download**: No taps; wait for disappearance (y≥1392 zone, do not tap there)
- **Login**:
  - **Username field**: Use `fill_credential_field` targeting near `Account, phone number, email` hint at (362, 1176) → the field is above the hint; use coordinate (362, 1176) as anchor (field directly above).
  - **Password field**: Near `Login PassWord` hint at (231, 1326) → field above the hint; coordinate (231, 1326) works.
  - **Login button**: After credential fill → `get_ocr_summary` to find `Log In` button coords in UI region (y<1392); then `tap_coordinate`. (Not the IME `Log In` at y~1860 – ignore.)
- **Server select**: Tap any server **name** (not header) → then `开始游戏` button (found via OCR, tap center).
- **In-game**: No further taps; `check_in_game` confirms.

## Pitfalls & tips

1. **GameTurbo overlay** (`GT[HK]: Xms S:Y`) always present; ignore.
2. **Download progress** appears in IME zone (y≥1392). **Do not tap** that region. Wait until progress text fully disappears (not just 100%).
3. **Login button** coords vary slightly; always re-OCR after credential fill to get exact `Log In` in y<1392 region before `tap_coordinate`.
4. **Server select** is mandatory after login – not covered in generic skill. Tap any server name, then `开始游戏`. If only one server, tap its row.
5. **`Loading main program`** is an intermediate stage after download; brief wait (3-8s) then login screen appears.
6. **`fill_credential_field`** works – use coordinates from field hint OCR. Both username and password fields found and filled successfully.
7. **No announcement modal** between privacy and download; if any unexpected popup, `analyze_screen`.
8. **`check_in_game`** may show errors (e.g. Katie tutorial text) but still confirms if `data.confirmed=true` with `main_screen_confirm_rounds` ≥2. Ignore non-blocking overlays.
